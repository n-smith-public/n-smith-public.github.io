Roxanne

Cast:

  • Singer (Any) - The "singer" of Roxanne

  • Guitarist (Any) - The "guitarist" of Roxanne

  • Drummer (Any) - The "percussionist" of Roxanne.

Based on Charlie Snow's original sketch, Roxanne

Filmed as a very generic 80s music video cover of Roxanne, by The Police. It would be in 4:3, and edited to look dated. Think like VHS overlay, static, screen tearing

The entire sketch takes place across campus with the cast "performing" Roxanne by The Police, except the sole audible word is Roxanne.

I imagine filming this under Rooftop, in the Alden Great Hall, on the fountain, and/or anywhere else that would be slightly unsettling.

While this could be done by people with actual instruments, it can also just be something like home depot buckets and a ukelele or something else like that.

During filming, the camera would be slightly out of focus, and randomly jump to different angles of the band, still facing forward. Ideally they would avoid blinking.

As the sketch goes on, it slowly devolves into more horror-like. Think even more screen tearing, fucked up colour grading, faster cuts, more out of focus, handheld, camera held vertical even.

Around 2/3rds of the way through the song, Singer suddenly "shoots" Drummer while facing away from the camera. They turn around to reveal they are wearing a Bane mask.

The Guitarist and Singer keep going as normal, ignoring that the Drummer now lies dead.

A little bit later, the Singer suddenly starts singing "Batman" instead of "Roxanne", before then "shooting" Guitarist as well.

After shooting Guitarist, Singer slowly starts to walk towards the camera while continuing to sing without any supporting instrumentalists. Once he gets close enough, the camera suddenly cuts to black.
