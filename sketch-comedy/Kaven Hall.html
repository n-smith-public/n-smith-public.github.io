Cast:

  • Person 1 (Any) - Just your average WPI student. Preferably comfortable singing, does not have to be good.

When2Meet

Costuming

Person 1

  • Shirt: Anything that feels 2010s, Vine video vibe.
  • Pants: See above
  • Accessories: Anything that fits, I have no preferences.

    • This is based on the parody I Walk to Burger King, which is based on the Green Day song Boulevard of Broken Dreams. The rythm of the singing should match the rhythm of the opening bit.

    Open on Person 1 walking up to Kaven Hall. The time is morning.

    Person 1 (singing in post) - I walk to Kaven Hall. then I walk back home from Kaven Hall.

    Person 1 is now inside of Kaven Hall.

    Person 1(singing in post) - Don't know what is here, who the hells a Civil Engineer?

    Person 1 is now leaving of Kaven Hall.

    Person 1 (singing in post) - I walked to Kaven Hall, will I walk back home from Kaven Hall?



    Cut to Person 1, now walking into Kaven Hall, Again. However, the time is in the afternoon.

    Person 1 (singing in post) - I walk to Goddard Hall. then I walk back home from Goddard Hall.

    Person 1 is now inside of Kaven Hall.

    Person 1 (singing in post) - I can't walk there, sometimes a ceiling tile will fall*

    Person 1 is now leaving of Kaven Hall.

    Person 1 (singing in post) - I walk to Goddard Hall, I can't get back home from Goddard Hall.



    Cut to Person 1, now walking into DAKA. The time is in the night.

    Person 1(singing, Note: NOT in post) - I walk to DAKA...

    Person 1 suddenly stops singing. The camera suddenly switches from Tripod to Shakey Cam. Person 1 starts breathing heavily, as they slowly turn around to see a Starship robot appear. Cut to black.



    * Workshop during filming.
