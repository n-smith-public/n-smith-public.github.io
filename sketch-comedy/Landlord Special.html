Open on a closed exterior door. LANDLORD is unlocking the door, while ROOMMATE 1 and ROOMMATE 2 are standing behind the camera.

LANDLORD - And this is the place.

ROOMMATE 1 - Oh wow, look at that nice shade of Mustard Yellow (Or similarly off colour, could be the actual colour of the walls, or the complete opposite.)... wait,. what's that?

ROOMMATE 2 - I don't think thats... (Gets cut off)

LANDLORD - And if you follow me down the hallway, you'll see the first bedroom.

Camera moves down the hall as the trio goes further into the apartment, keeping the 3 in shot while hiding anything else.

ROOMMATE 1 - So... spacious.

Camera rotates to show what is essentially a closet. Ideally, this is an actual closet, and is either completely empty, or stuffed to the brim.

LANDLORD - Yup, it even has a window! A rare find around here.

ROOMMATE 1 - Does it come with the carpet?

The closet should not have a carpet.

LANDLORD - Nope. (Pause) Well, now if you look across the hallway, there's the kitchen. Recently remodeled.

Camera turns around, avoiding showing the remainder of the hallway, and turns to a kitchen. There should be a hole at about eye level w/ the camera. Can either be an actual hole, or a black circle taped onto the wall.

ROOMMATE 2 - Hmm... I'm pretty sure I just saw... (Gets cut off, again)

LANDLORD - And that would be your complimentary pet. Keep in mind that this is the only pet allowed on the lease.

ROOMMATE 1 - Is it friendly?

LANDLORD - Only if you feed it cheese.

LANDLORD - And there's the other bedroom, but that's boring. Instead, take a look at the pristine bathroom. (Add extra enthesis on the pristine)

Camera turns back towards the people during this, where we follow them go to another room, where the camera turns around to an empty room with just a single bucket. Ideally, the lights will be off in this room.

ROOMMATE 1 - Awesome!

ROOMMATE 2 - Is this even legal?

LANDLORD - Of course it is! Unfortunately, the shower is currently being renovated. For now, you will need to go upstairs and ask your neighbors to borrow theirs.

ROOMMATE 1 - Sounds normal, I had to do that back in (Insert city that isn't Worcester.)

ROOMMATE 2 - Several. First off... (Cut off again)

ROOMMATE 1 - Nope! When can we sign?

LANDLORD - I have the lease right here.
