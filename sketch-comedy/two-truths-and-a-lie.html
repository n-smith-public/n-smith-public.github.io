Two(?) Truths and a Lie

Cast

▾

    Solder 1 (Any) - A truth teller

    Adhesive 2 (Any) - A lie maker

    Masion (Any) - An approacher

Solder and Adhesive are standing between two doors, guarding them.

Masion approaches the two

Masion (before either of them can speak) - So, do one of you always tell the truth and the other only lies?

Solder - Yes

Adhesive - No

A beat, then Masion and Adhesive turn to look at each other

Cut to black
